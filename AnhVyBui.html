IT interest

Moreover, the IT industry has a great job potential when graduating and have good income. I have become interesting in computer when I am in grade 8. At that time, I had to take a job exam to prepare for the exam when I finished grade 9 and computer science was compulsory subject in my school. I studied about Word, Excel, Powerpoint, Pascal and how to create a basic website. Then I found computer and science is very useful and decided to choose IT when I applied for the university entrance exam.
